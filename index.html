"Profile"

Experienced Materials Science Researcher and Project Coordinator

Dr. Mansour is a results-oriented professional with a diverse background in materials science, engineering and project coordination. In his current role as Project Coordinator for Outreach and Training, he leads initiatives to support the scientific community in adopting and maintaining high standards of research data management practices based on the FAIR Data Principles. Dr. Mansour is also contributing to the development of a federated and FAIR infrastructure for materials science data.

He has extensive research expertise in:

  • Organic semiconductors: Tuning and characterisation of electrical, optical and electronic properties via molecular doping.
  • Carbon-related materials: Synthesis of graphene and diamond-like carbon, optimisation of graphene growth and chemical modification for transparent conductive electrodes.
  • CVD and Ultra High Vacuum Systems: Design, troubleshooting and maintenance of chemical vapour deposition and ultra high vacuum systems for experimental research.

A certified Project Management Professional (PMPÂ®), Dr Mansour excels at driving projects to success and fostering international collaboration. His remarkable leadership skills are evidenced by his management of numerous projects and his active involvement in extra-curricular activities.

Dr. Mansour is an active member of the Materials Research Society (MRS) and a founding officer of the KAUST MRS Student Chapter - the first international MRS student chapter. Known for his strong oral and written communication skills, he excels at effectively communicating complex concepts to diverse audiences. Dr Mansour thrives on achievement, learning opportunities and problem solving, and uses his highly organised nature to achieve exceptional results in all his endeavours.
