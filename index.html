Expertise:
​Experienced researcher in tuning and characterizing the electrical, optical and electronic properties of organic semiconductors via molecular doping. In addition to the synthesis of carbon-related materials such as graphene and diamond like carbon. Design and troubleshooting of CVD systems, optimization of graphene growth, and its chemical modification towards application as transparent conductive electrode. Advanced user of ultrahigh vacuum systems including installation, maintenance and running experiments.  Remarkable leadership practice developed through heading of numerous projects and extracurricular activities. Dr. Mansour is an active member of the Materials Research Society (MRS), and a founding officer of the KAUST-MRS student chapter, the first international MRS student chapter in the world.
